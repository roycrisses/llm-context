## 2025-05-15 - [Delightful CLI Feedback and Ergonomics]
**Learning:** Adding descriptive emojis and ensuring immediate feedback for long-running operations (like LLM requests) significantly improves the perceived responsiveness and scannability of a CLI tool. Defaulting to the current directory follows the principle of least effort for the most common user path.
**Action:** Always consider sensible defaults for CLI arguments and use visual cues (emojis, color) to distinguish between different stages of a multi-step process.
