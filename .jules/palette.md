## 2025-05-15 - Enhance CLI feedback and defaults
**Learning:** CLI tools feel more "alive" and delightful when they provide visual feedback (emojis) and smart defaults (defaulting to current directory). Providing specific metadata (file/token counts) in success messages builds trust and provides immediate value to the user.
**Action:** Use prefix-matching for emojis in CLI helpers and ensure all terminal actions report their impact (counts/tokens).
